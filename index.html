About INTEG

Built on Engineering Precision

INTEG is a specialty MEP contractor with 8+ years of proven performance in the Florida market. We self-perform mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and medical gas work with a team of licensed engineers and certified technicians.

Our PreFab-first approach delivers greater quality control, reduced field risk, and faster project completion. From pre-construction planning beyond Florida Building Code compliance to BIM coordination at LOD 350 — we own every phase.

Single point of accountability from design through commissioning. That's why Jackson Health System, AC Marriott, and Florida's top developers choose INTEG.
