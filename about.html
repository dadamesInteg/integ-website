Who We Are

Specialty MEP Contractor. Florida-Focused.

INTEG Integral Engineering is a specialty MEP contractor serving healthcare and commercial clients across Florida. We self-perform across all four MEP disciplines — mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and building automation — under a single contract, with one team and one point of contact.

We were founded by qualified engineers with a forward-thinking, problem-solving approach. Over 8 years in the US market, we've built a reputation for technical precision, reliable delivery, and deep expertise in life-safety systems — the kind that hospitals and high-rise developers depend on.

From Jackson Health System's critical care expansions to AC Marriott's VRF mechanical systems — INTEG delivers on spec, on time, and on code.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Technical Precision

Every system we engineer meets code — and then some. We apply Florida Building Code, NFPA, and ASHRAE standards as a baseline, not a ceiling.

Full Accountability

One contract. One team. One point of contact from design through commissioning. No finger-pointing between subcontractors — we own the outcome.

On-Time Delivery

Our PreFab-first methodology and BIM coordination at LOD 350 keep projects on schedule by eliminating field surprises before they happen.

Client-First Approach

We begin every project by understanding your specific needs — not applying a standard template. Your facility's requirements drive every engineering decision.

Life-Safety Focus

Healthcare systems are life-critical. We treat every medical gas pipe, HVAC pressurization zone, and BMS alarm as if patient outcomes depend on it — because they do.

Trust & Reliability

75%+ of our work comes from repeat clients and referrals. We earn that trust project by project — through transparent communication and consistent execution.
